Who am I?

My name is Craig Tetley and I'm happy to see your interested in learning the fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-jitsu!

I started Brazilian Jiu-jitsu after suffering many years of bullying and realising that flashy karate and kickboxing wasn't helping me in the schoolyard scraps taking place!

I spent the last 15 years training with Victor Braulio learning BJJ with Gracie Barra. Over the last 10 years I've been further developing it and teaching it. I'm going to share how I can pass my knowledge to you. I hope it changes your life for the better, just like it changed mine!

I’m sure most of you have a vague idea as to what the essence of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is with the popular UFC.....it’s a grappling art! One that’s designed to take your opponent to the ground, control them, dominate them and then execute a joint-lock or choke! It’s a very challenging yet rewarding martial art! It has all the obvious benefits we hear people speak of....(just a quick Google search will tell anyone that) Some of them include it being part of a self defence system...(hence why law enforcement go through programs derived from Jiu Jitsu) its great for building our confidence, helping us become stronger, fitter and leaner versions of ourselves without even realising we’re working hard. But now, more than ever, I think we need to recognise and appreciate all the many other benefits BJJ can offer us. The silent benefits that not many people focus on....but the things we now realise are important since covid struck and Lockdown happened! The things that matter most....like forming connections and making friends, managing our stress levels, teaching us ways to deal with pressure, to get more active and healthier, doing something for fun and for the soul, something to challenge our minds and help grow us mentally, help increase our energy levels. We as a club are very passionate that BJJ can bring all this and more to anyone that chooses the journey. Now, when we all need it the most! It really is a great vehicle to help us improve and enrich our life’s!

As said above BJJ is a great stress reliever. Stress is responsible for causing many illnesses and negative impacts on our lives. Many people that practice BJJ talk of it being their ‘therapy!’ When you step on that mat, the focus you have to have makes everything else fade away! Giving us that much needed mental respite! The competitive nature of the art allows us to ‘blow off steam’ too....in a very controlled and safe environment! Most people have been leading sedentary life’s this past year or more....practising BJJ relieves our stress but also reenergises us! I know it sounds counter-intuitive to ‘use’ more energy to ‘get’ more energy...but trust us...it’s works!

In relation to friends and connections! There’s something to be said about how trying to control and submit a person, actually allows us to form deeper bonds with our teammates !!! Something about BJJ allows great camaraderie....and it’s not just the friends you make on the mat....it’s the wider BJJ community that exists outside your club too!!! EXACTLY WHAT YOU ARE GETTING

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is an extremely effective martial art and is, when done well, an art that allows the smaller person to use concepts and leverage to control and subdue the larger and stronger opponent. In this beginners course you will learn the art of Jiu Jitsu, you will learn how to take your opponent down safely to the floor and then control them once they are there. You will learn why the hips are so important in Jiu Jitsu, in attack and defence. You will learn how to take and hold dominant positions of control, then isolate limbs for joint locks. You will learn of the most dominant position in Jiu Jitsu, ‘the back’ and how to use submissions from this position. Submissions such as the Rear Naked choke, romantically nicknamed the ‘Lion Killer’.....giving you a vivid image of its powerful capability. You will learn also how to escape from bad situations, so you will become confident no matter where the fight is. Then finally, you will learn the thing that Jiu Jitsu is most known for...The Guard. This is the ability to fight when your back is on the floor and your opponent is on top. You will learn how to sweep and submit your opponent from this position. This is an extremely important position to learn, it has serious self-defence implications, and also is the thing that builds the most confidence. We will show you how to take the worst case scenario in a fight and make it your strength. Just what Jiu Jitsu was intended for!!!
